Frequently asked questions

About the site and the tools

Are these calculators free to use?

Yes. The tools run in the browser and are free to use for quick planning estimates.

Do the calculators use my data?

Results stay in your browser unless you choose to copy or share them.

Are the results exact?

They are planning estimates. Real payroll, tax, lender, or platform details can change the exact numbers.

Which calculator should I start with?

Start with the tool that matches your question: hourly pay, salary, freelance rate, budget, savings, emergency fund, debt payoff, or side-hustle profit.
