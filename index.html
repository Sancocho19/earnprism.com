A money calculator site built for real search intent and longer sessions.

Instead of chasing impossible broad keywords, this site starts with tools that solve concrete money questions: hourly pay, freelance rates, creator income, raises, budgets, and savings goals. That creates clearer intent, stronger internal linking, and cleaner monetization paths.

Why this is better than a generic viral website

A generic website competes with giant publishers without a concrete reason to rank. A calculator hub solves specific tasks, creates repeatable page templates, and gives users a reason to click deeper.

Direct search intent

Users arrive with a specific question and get an answer immediately.

Easy to expand

You can clone the structure into new calculator categories without rebuilding the whole site.

High sharing potential

Salary, raise, and savings results are naturally screenshot-friendly and easy to share.

Clean monetization

Ads and affiliates can sit in natural places without breaking the calculator experience.

What to build next after launch

Add long-tail pages like paycheck planning, contractor vs employee calculators, debt payoff tools, emergency fund calculators, and geo-targeted variants only after the first cluster is indexed.

What not to do

Do not launch with 100 thin pages. Start with a compact cluster of pages that actually help the user and create strong internal pathways.
