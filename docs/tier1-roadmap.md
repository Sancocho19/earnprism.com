# Tier 1 roadmap

## Positioning
Start with English-only pages and USD-first defaults so the site matches higher-value advertiser markets from day one.

## Phase 1: launch this cluster
- Hourly wage to salary
- Freelance rate calculator
- Creator income calculator
- Raise calculator
- 50/30/20 budget calculator
- Savings goal calculator
- 3 supporting guides

## Phase 2: expand only after indexing
Add one tight cluster at a time:
- emergency fund calculator
- debt payoff calculator
- contractor vs employee calculator
- side hustle income calculator
- paycheck breakdown explainer
- negotiation templates or checklists

## Phase 3: monetization
- display ads once traffic quality is clear
- finance and payroll affiliates
- email capture with budgeting or pricing templates
- sponsored placements only after the site feels trustworthy
