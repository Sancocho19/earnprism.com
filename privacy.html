Privacy

This page explains how the site handles basic data and browser storage.

Last updated: April 2026

Local browser storage

Some calculators may store recent results or recently opened tools in local browser storage so the site can remember them on the same device. This helps the tools feel faster and more convenient. That information stays in the browser unless you choose to copy or share it.

Analytics, ads, and third-party tools

If analytics, advertising, affiliate links, or email tools are used on this site, they may collect data according to their own policies. Where applicable, this page should be read together with any consent or cookie notices shown on the site.

Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time as the site changes. The latest version published on this page should be treated as the current one.

Contact

For privacy questions related to the site, use the contact details provided on the site when available.
