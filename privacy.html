Privacy

Learn what EarnPrism stores locally in your browser, how the site handles simple calculator data, and how to reach the site with privacy questions.

Updated April 2026Site policyPrivacy & browser data

Privacy overview

EarnPrism is a simple content and calculator site built for practical money planning. The site does not require a user account to use the main tools.

Calculator data

Calculator inputs and recent results are stored locally in the browser on the device being used so the site can show recent tools and copied results more conveniently. That local storage does not create a server-side account by itself.

Third-party services

If EarnPrism begins using analytics, advertising, affiliate, or email providers, this page should name those services clearly and explain what role they play on the site.

Contact

For privacy questions or correction requests, contact support@earnprism.com.

What this page means in practice

Most people use EarnPrism without logging in, and the main tools work from the browser. The most common data behavior on the site is local browser storage for recent tool use and copied results, not account creation.

If the site later adds cookies, analytics, ads, affiliate tools, or email capture, this page should be updated again to reflect the live setup.
