Privacy Policy

This page explains the basic privacy practices used on EarnPrism.

Information we may collect

EarnPrism may collect standard website analytics such as page views, device type, approximate location, referrer, and on-site behavior through analytics tools. The site may also store recent calculator activity in your browser so returning visitors can reopen tools more easily.

Cookies and local storage

This site may use cookies, analytics tags, and browser storage to improve performance, remember recent activity, and measure how pages are used. Third-party services such as analytics, advertising, or affiliate tools may also use their own cookies where applicable.

Advertising and affiliate relationships

If EarnPrism displays ads, sponsored placements, newsletter signups, or affiliate links, those services may collect data according to their own privacy policies. Users should review those external policies when interacting with third-party offers.

How information is used

Information may be used to improve the site, understand which tools are helpful, monitor performance, prevent abuse, and present relevant content or offers.

Your choices

You can limit cookies through your browser settings and clear local storage at any time. Depending on your location, you may also have legal rights to request access, correction, or deletion of personal data.

Contact

For privacy questions, add a real contact email before launch, for example: privacy@earnprism.com.
