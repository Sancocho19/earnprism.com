About EarnPrism

EarnPrism is a practical money tools site focused on salary, freelance pricing, creator income, budgeting, savings goals, and raise planning.

What you can do here

Use calculators to turn hourly pay into salary estimates, set a freelance rate floor, estimate creator income, measure a raise, build a simple 50/30/20 budget, and work toward a savings target.

How the site works

Each tool is designed to be quick, readable, and useful on mobile. Results are paired with plain-English explanations and related guides so users can move from a number to a next step.

Who it is for

EarnPrism is for employees, freelancers, creators, students, and anyone trying to make better decisions around income and money planning.

Important note

The tools on this site are planning aids and educational estimates. They are not tax, payroll, legal, accounting, or investment advice.
