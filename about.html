What EarnPrism is for

EarnPrism is a practical money-planning site focused on the questions people ask when they need numbers behind real decisions: how much a job pays month to month, what a raise changes after tax, how to set a sustainable freelance rate, how long debt payoff may take, and how a savings plan can look over time.

What makes the site useful

The site is built around calculators and short supporting guides that work together. The calculator gives a fast estimate. The guide gives context, limitations, and a better next step. That structure is meant to reduce vague advice and replace it with a clearer path.

What EarnPrism is not

It is not payroll software, tax software, legal advice, or lending advice. It is a planning layer designed to help users ask better questions and compare options more clearly.
