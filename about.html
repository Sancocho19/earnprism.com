About this project

IncomeGrid is a starter calculator site designed for a clean Tier 1 launch. The model is simple: solve useful money questions, create strong internal pathways, and expand only after early pages prove themselves.

Why this site format

This project favors practical tools over broad magazine-style content. Calculator pages match search intent well, are easier to scale, and create obvious next clicks into guides and related tools.

How to customize it

Replace the placeholder domain, add your brand assets, connect Search Console, and update the legal pages with your real business details before launch.

How to grow it safely

Do not flood the site with thin pages. Add new calculators in clusters so your internal links stay tight and the site keeps a clear topic focus.

Before publishing

  • Change the domainReplace every instance of yourdomain.com with your real domain.
  • Update legal infoAdd your company or owner details to privacy and terms pages.
  • Track indexingSubmit the sitemap in Google Search Console.
