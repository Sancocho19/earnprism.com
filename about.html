About EarnPrism

EarnPrism is a simple library of calculators and guides built to make everyday money decisions easier to compare and easier to act on.

What you will find here

EarnPrism focuses on practical questions that come up often in real life: converting hourly pay into monthly income, comparing salary offers, estimating overtime, setting freelance rates, quoting projects, building an emergency fund, planning savings goals, and understanding debt payoff timelines.

How to use the site

Start with the calculator that matches the question you have right now. Once the number is clear, open the related guide or second tool that helps you make the next decision. The site is designed to work as a planning companion, not as a replacement for payroll, tax, legal, or investment advice.

What the results are for

The tools are built for estimates, scenario testing, and better planning conversations. Real paychecks, tax withholding, lender statements, contracts, and platform rules can produce different outcomes. Use the results to think more clearly, compare options, and plan the next step with fewer blind spots.
