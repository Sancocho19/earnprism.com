Frequently asked questions

Why include inflation?

Because a raise that sounds good can still be weak if prices rise almost as fast as your salary.

Does this calculate exact payroll tax?

No. It uses the tax rate you enter, so it is an estimate for planning rather than payroll accounting.

What is the best number to focus on?

Usually the net monthly increase, because it is the easiest to use in real life.
