Frequently asked questions

Does this guarantee investment returns?

No. It only applies the annual rate you enter to show a simple estimate.

Can I start from zero?

Yes. Enter a starting balance of zero and a monthly contribution amount.

Why is this useful even without interest?

Because most people need a timeline first. The saving habit matters before optimization.
