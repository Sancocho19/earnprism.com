Why overtime changes the math

Overtime can meaningfully raise weekly income, but it can also create irregular pay. This tool helps separate what came from normal hours and what came from extra time.

Check the policy first

Overtime rules vary by employer, location, and contract. Use the multiplier that matches your real situation, then treat the result as a planning estimate.

Use it with salary planning

If overtime is frequent, use this page alongside the budget calculator so your fixed monthly costs do not rely too heavily on extra hours.

Good follow-up question

After getting the number, ask what decision this result should help with next: comparing offers, quoting work, setting a budget, building a fund, or speeding up debt payoff.
