What this calculator helps with

Turn an hourly rate into a monthly and yearly income estimate. This is one of the cleanest search-intent pages because people usually want an answer fast and then move to budgeting or savings.

How to read the result

The result is an estimate for planning, not payroll. Overtime, unpaid time off, bonuses, and taxes can all change the real number. The point is to convert an abstract hourly figure into something useful for day-to-day money decisions.

Frequently asked questions

Does this use gross or net pay?

It uses the hourly number you enter. If your number is pre-tax, the result is gross. If your number is already after tax, the estimate reflects that.

Can I use it for part-time work?

Yes. Change the hours per week and paid weeks per year to match your real schedule.

Why convert to monthly pay?

Because rent, groceries, debt payments, and most budgeting decisions are made on a monthly basis.
