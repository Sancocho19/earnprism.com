Profit is the useful number

Revenue can look exciting, but profit is what matters for planning. This page helps separate sales from what is actually left after fees, taxes, and recurring costs.

Good for creators and freelancers too

Any small income stream with platform fees or software costs can use this page, even if it is not a classic side hustle.

Use it for decisions

A clean profit estimate makes it easier to decide whether to reinvest, save, pay debt, or scale slowly.

Good follow-up question

After getting the number, ask what decision this result should help with next: comparing offers, quoting work, setting a budget, building a fund, or speeding up debt payoff.

Frequently asked questions

Should I reserve taxes here?

Usually yes. Setting money aside early can make the business feel more real and lower surprises later.

What goes in recurring costs?

Subscriptions, tools, hosting, shipping software, storage, or any monthly operating cost.

Can I use it for seasonal income?

Yes. Run several monthly scenarios instead of relying on one average.
