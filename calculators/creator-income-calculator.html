The output is a planning estimate, not a promise. Real creator income changes by niche, platform, brand fit, and how well offers convert. Still, it gives users a far better baseline than guessing.

Frequently asked questions

Is this a guaranteed creator income number?

No. It is a planning estimate based on the inputs you enter, not a guarantee of future earnings.

Why use engagement and not only followers?

Because engaged audiences are usually more valuable for sponsorships and direct sales than raw follower count alone.

Can small creators use this too?

Yes. It is often more useful for smaller creators because they need better benchmarks to price offers realistically.
